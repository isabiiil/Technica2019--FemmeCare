#BUILYBYGIRLS

#BUILTBYGIRLS gives high school and college women access to real Advisors, exclusive events at top tech companies, and no-fluff resources to help them explore and land their first internship or job.

Their mission is to prepare young women everywhere to claim their place in the careers, industries, and roles they want - starting with the tech industry.

DECRIMNOW

DECRIMNOW is a campaign and movement to decriminalize sex work and to promote the well-being and safety of people in the sex trade.

DECRIMNOW DC was created by the Sex Worker Advocates Coalition (SWAC), a group of individuals and organizations promoting the human rights, civil rights and liberties, health, safety, and well-being of sex workers and people profiled as sex workers in Washington, DC while advocating for legislation and other policy changes to challenge the system of criminalization of sex work.

The Period Collective

The Period Collective is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization, committed to supplying feminine hygiene products to homeless and low-income women in the greater Chicago area.
Our organization's focus is on supplying products to shelters, traditional housing facilities, food banks, schools, prisons and other organizations that work with homeless and low-income girls and women. We are aiming to fill a void, as feminine hygiene products are not frequently being donated and organizations working to help these women do not have a budget for supplying these items.
